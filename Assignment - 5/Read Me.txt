Sample Dataset has been provided which fulfills every condition

i) Roll-Number {18L1863}, where the letter indicates the campus i.e. L = Lahore,
   I = Islamabad, K = Karachi, F = Faisalabad, P = Peshawar

ii) Subject

iii) Grade per Subject

The thing to note is that we can have the following things, 

i) Duplicate roll_numbers but either with same subject and same grade or same subject 
   and different grade or different subject and different grade
ii) Students are not only in the range from 1995 - 2018, but more to help us filter
iii) The data is not ordered in any way.
